About Us

Who We Are

The National English Language and Literature Quiz (NELLIQ) -Educational Network - Ghana is an independent educational initiative established by education professionals and examiners with the vision of promoting literacy, communication excellence, literary appreciation, and critical thinking among young people.

Our Vision

To become Ghana’s premier English Language and Literature academic platform.

Our Mission

To promote excellence in language, literature, communication, and reading culture.

Core Values

Excellence Integrity Innovation Inclusiveness Professionalism

Why NELLIQ?

Addressing Core Challenges:

  • Declining reading culture
  • Weak communication skills
  • Poor literary engagement
  • Low interest in Literature
  • Limited national platforms for language excellence

Educational Impact & Outcomes:

  • Analytical thinking development
  • Communication confidence
  • Leadership cultivation
  • Enhanced creativity
  • Revived robust reading culture
